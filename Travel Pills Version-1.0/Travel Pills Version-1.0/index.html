Overview

Counseling you before travel

During Pre travel counseling our well-organized and well-executed pre-travel consultation program gathers basic information and do appropriate health risk assessment and management concerning the trip planned. We accordingly advise the traveler customized care based on his itinerary, health and pre medical history.

This helps us to provide consistent, appropriate, and efficient pre-travel health preparation for each individual.

Support during travel

We provide you onsite upfront medical support through phone, email and SMS by which you can be in touch with us during your travel. For our travelers if needed we can also provide your onsite nearest health care provider with your previous health care records.

Emergency health kit

A travel health kit is important no matter where you travel. We customize travel health kit which contains enough supplies to prevent illness, handle minor injuries and illnesses, and manage pre-existing medical conditions according to traveler’s needs, type of travel, length of travel, and destination

We pack medications in their original, clearly labeled containers with a signed and dated letter from our physician describing your medical conditions and medications which will be very useful on travel.
